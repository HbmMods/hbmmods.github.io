Fusion Reactor Overhaul

  • Torus with a recipe selector, fuel combinations no longer require a dedicated plasma fluid
  • Instead of an assembled microwave, the initial power comes from a pseudo-multiblock klystron
  • Power generation is now outsourced to attachments, either via boiler (SDS) or MHD turbine
  • Breeding is now an extenral attachment
  • Optional byproduct catcher attachment increases the rate with which the torus creates byproduct
  • Coupler turns output energy into usable input energy, allowing toruses to be chained
  • Possibly other plasma powered machines? Plasma welding?
